E-RAPIID Application


Django powered application for personal disclosure for X-BANK. The "ELON Request Application for personal Interests, Investments and disclosures" ( E-RAPIID) is a web based system that enables X BANK to meet regulatory and Compliance requirements on employees' personal account trading, Personal Investments and Outside affiliations. This application provide platform to Employees, Manager and Compliance to do declare and forecast details.
